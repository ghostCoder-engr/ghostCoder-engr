About This Website

                
This website helps you easily calculate exponential growth/decay and temperature changes over time. Whether you’re a student, professional, or just curious, this tool is simple and accurate.

                
                
Features

                
    
                    
  • Growth/Decay Calculator: Solve problems like population growth, radioactive decay, or compound interest.
    • 
                    
  • Cooling/Heating Calculator: Predict temperature changes using Newton’s Law of Cooling and Heating.
    • 
                    
  • Step-by-Step Solutions: See how the calculations are done for better understanding.
    • 
                    
  • User-Friendly Design: Works on any device and ensures accurate inputs.
    • 
                

                
                
Limitations

                
    
                    
  • No graphs or batch calculations yet.
    • 
                    
  • Cannot handle complex numbers or negative time values.
    • 
                    
  • Depends on accurate user inputs for results.
    • 
                    
  • No option to choose units or perform unit conversions.
    • 
                    
  • Cannot calculate more than one missing value at a time.
